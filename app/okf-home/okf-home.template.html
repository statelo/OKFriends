About OKF

Overseas Korean Friends

OKFriends, as an organization affiliated with the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, we work and communicate with overseas Koreans. Our aim is to inform and introduce Korean culture as well as Korean language through cultural and international exchange with Koreans living Overseas.
